This is a respectful tribute page. It contains a brief overview, messages of remembrance, and links to resources for learning about systemic racism and ways to get involved.

“I can't breathe.” — A phrase that sparked global calls for justice and reform.

Overview

George Floyd's death in 2020 became a turning point in conversations about policing, justice, and racial inequality worldwide. This page aims to remember, reflect, and point visitors toward constructive resources.

Ways to Learn or Help

  • Read documented reporting and reliable news coverage about the events and subsequent reforms.
  • Support community organizations working on criminal justice reform.
  • Educate yourself about systemic racism and anti-racism resources.
